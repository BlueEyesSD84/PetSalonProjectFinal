About the developer

I have been working in the IT industry for over 20+ years. In those years I mainly worked as an IT Tech, specifically working on equipment.

Prior to working in the IT industry, I was in the U.S. Navy for 20 years. I worked mainly on Aircraft Carriers on the flight deck as an Aviation Boatswain's Mate. If you've ever seen the movie 'Top Gun' at the beginning of the movie, there is a guy wearing a yellow coat waiving his hands directing the aircarft around the flight deck. That's what I did for the Navy.

I decided my passion is web design. I've always felt great satisfaction in being able to type code and have it product instant results. In addion, I enjoy being able to make people's ideas into action on the web. I am training at San Diego Global Knowledge University to become a Full Stack Developer.
